Overview

K1LADEX ("K1LADEX," "we," "our," or "us") operates k1ladex.com (the "Site") and app.k1ladex.com (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the Site or App, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • Usage data: Ticker symbols entered into the scan engine, scan frequency, and general usage patterns. This data is not tied to your identity.
  • Scan count: A scan counter is stored in your browser's localStorage. This stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store your credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy governs that data.
  • Email address: If you contact us by email, we retain your address to respond to your inquiry.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL, timestamps) may be retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation to use the free tier.
  • We do not collect your name, address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide them.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising networks.

Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses browser localStorage to store your free scan count and session state. No tracking cookies are set by K1LADEX. If you use a third-party payment link (Stripe), Stripe may set cookies governed by their policy.

Third-Party Services

The App fetches live market data from third-party providers including Finnhub and Finviz. When the App requests data on your behalf, your query (the ticker symbol) is transmitted to those services. Review Finnhub's and Finviz's privacy policies for their data handling practices.

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. K1LADEX does not store payment card data.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to support inquiries

  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

Data Retention

Server logs are retained for up to 30 days. Email correspondence is retained until you request deletion. Scan data is not retained beyond the current session at the server level.
